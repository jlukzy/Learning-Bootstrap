Filling Every Occassion With Great Food And Service

What We Do

Our goal is to ensure that your event thrills the senses and delights the palate. You’ll also experience peace of mind, because, simply put: the company behind it all cares.

Explore Great Taste With Us

Lunches that please the palate and have real nutritional value can spark the appetite and energize mind and body for the afternoon ahead. JoeJoe's Caters makes beyond-the-box lunches from organic, sustainably sourced ingredients, using eco-friendly practices. Fresh, homemade, baked to order and delivered on time – everybody loves lunch when it’s this easy.

Why Choose Us?

We’ve worked hard to establish a coast-to-coast standard of excellence that supports award-winning sustainable practices in our kitchens. The strong relationships we have developed with local growers and farmers help support our relationships with the most important people of all – our clients. With organic and sustainable practices second nature to our business, JoeJoe's Caters can devote its full attention to the values, taste and preferences of each individual client. From the initial planning phase, we will work collaboratively with you to ensure your event reaches well beyond its full potential.
